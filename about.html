Our Mission

Navsanjeevan Social Trust (NST) is dedicated to empowering marginalized communities in India. We achieve this by providing access to quality education, vital skills training, and creating opportunities for sustainable livelihoods. Recognizing the disparity between rural and urban populations, NST strives to bridge this gap by ensuring everyone has access to the resources they need to thrive. Ultimately, our mission is to foster a society built on inclusivity and sustainability, where every individual has the chance to reach their full potential.

Our Programs

The Navsanjeevan Social Trust works to empower individuals and communities in Pune and surrounding districts. Through the Youth Skill Development program, they equip students with market-ready skills by offering Pre-vocational Education (6th-8th Std) and Multi Skill Foundation courses (9th-12th Std) that emphasize employability skills through internships. Additionally, vocational training programs cater specifically to agricultural trades, ensuring a skilled workforce prepared for local job market needs. Career guidance and counseling are provided in 5 schools, supporting students in 10th and 12th grades as they make informed decisions about their futures. The Scholarship Program tackles educational barriers by providing financial aid for technical education to underprivileged individuals, aiming to increase the number of skilled professionals. Beyond schools, the Trust's Women Empowerment initiatives empower women in 51 villages through self-help group formation, training programs reaching 3,000 women, and a strong focus on promoting gender equality and improving livelihoods. Their dedication to sustainable practices extends to agriculture as well, with training programs equipping 300 small farmers with modern techniques. Finally, their community development efforts prioritize the well-being of residents, exemplified by establishing an RO water treatment plant that benefits over 2,700 families in the Kolhapur district.
