Navsanjeevan Social Trust is a right-based, behavior-change organisation addressing complicated issues in Indian society. It creates and implements developmental processes and training. Community members, including women and youth, can benefit from technology and skill development. Navsanjeevan Social Trust collaborates with State Governments, Local NGO's, and domain experts to operate in 51 villages, 10 schools, and engage 4136 community people and 665 students.Levels of Interventions: Community/village Our action-oriented approach empowers 51 communities by strengthening the ecosystem for community welfare and implementing robust mechanisms to improve education delivery in the district. This includes skill development and intensive community engagement. Action at 10 schools with 665 pupils aimed to increase and develop skills.

RECENT ACTIVITIES & EVENTS

Our recent activities and events

Youth Development

Navsanjeevan Social Trust focuses on youth development through skill enhancement programs. They engage youth in various initiatives to foster growth and provide opportunities for positive contributions to society. By organizing development programs, Navsanjeevan nurtures young talents, empowering them to become active and responsible community members.

Women Empowerment

(Self-help groups) Navsanjeevan social trust works with women for the overall development of women through the self-help groups since 2013. A total of 51 villages and 250 Self-help groups were formed and training was provided to 3000 women. Navsanjeevan social trust works in close collaboration with the Government department to provide different types of training programs to Self-help groups. The organization helps the women to start small skills industries as well as provides technical support for business proposal preparation etc. to start their own businesses.

Women Awareness Programme

The Trust has organized various programs on women's rights in the district. This program is attended by SHG members or women participants. We educate the women about their fundamental rights and also educate women in rural and urban areas.
